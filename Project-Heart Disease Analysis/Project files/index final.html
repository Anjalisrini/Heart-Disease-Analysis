Analysis Of Heart Disease

Heart Disease is a general term that includes many types of heart problems. It's also called Cardiovascular disease, which means heart and blood vessel diseases.Heart Diseases include blood vessel disease, irregular heart beats, disease of the heart muscle etc.

Causes..

High Blood Pressure, Diabetics, Smoking, Alcohol Drinking, physical inactivity, Unhealthy Diet

Preventions

Healthy Diet, Physical activity,Contol on Smoking ,Alcohol and Diabetic
